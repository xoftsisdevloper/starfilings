Sole Proprietorship

Best for: Freelancers, small traders, consultants. Single owner, unlimited liability but minimal compliance.

📋 Required documents:
  • PAN card (individual)
  • Aadhaar card / Voter ID
  • Bank account statement / cancelled cheque
  • Office address proof (rent agreement / utility bill)
  • Passport size photo

Process time: 2-4 working days.

Enquire on WhatsApp

Partnership Firm

Best for: Two or more partners running a business. Simple structure, partnership deed registered.

📋 Documents required:
  • Partnership deed (drafted on stamp paper)
  • PAN cards of all partners
  • Address proof of partners (Aadhaar/Voter ID)
  • Firm's registered office proof
  • Photographs of partners

GST registration included if applicable.

Chat on WhatsApp

Limited Liability Partnership (LLP)

Combines benefits of partnership and limited liability. Registered with MCA.

📁 Required documents:
  • DPAN for LLP
  • Identity proof of partners
  • Address proof of partners
  • Registered office proof
  • DSC (Digital signature) for designated partners
✨ We handle all MCA filings and incorporation certificate.
Get started on WhatsApp
