Documents Needed for GST Registration

Proprietor

  1. PAN Card
  2. AADHAR Card
  3. Passport Size Photo
  4. Name & Address Proof
  5. Rental Or Lease Agreement of Business Place
  6. Latest EB Bills of Business Place
  7. Property Tax of Business Place
  8. Name & Address Board of Business Place.

Partnership Firm

  1. Partnership Deed
  2. Partnership PAN Card
  3. Rental or Lease Agreement of Business Place
  4. Name & Address Board of Business Place
  5. PAN Card of each Partner
  6. Aadhar Card of each Partner
  7. Passport Size Photo of each Partner
  8. Name & Address Proof of each Partner
  9. Appointment Letter of Authorized Partner

Private Limited /LLP

  1. Certificate of Company Incorporation
  2. PAN Card of Company
  3. Memorandum of Association & Articles of Association
  4. Digital Signature Certificate
  5. Rental or Lease Agreement of Business Place
  6. Name & Address Board of Business Place
  7. PAN Card of each Director
  8. Aadhar Card of each Director
  9. Passport Size Photo of each Director
  10. Name & Address Proof of each Director
  11. Appointment Letter of Authorized Signatory
