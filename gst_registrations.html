GST registration is the process of enrolling a business under the Goods and Services Tax (GST) system in India. This registration enables businesses to collect and remit GST to the government. It is mandatory for businesses whose turnover exceeds the threshold limit set by authorities. The registration process involves submitting required documents and details online through the GST portal to obtain a unique GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number).
